  • what is white watar rafting?

    • Rafting or white water rafting is a challenging recreational activity using an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other bodies of water. This is usually done on white water or different degrees of rough water, in order to thrill and excite the raft passengers. The development of this activity as a leisure sport has become popular since the mid-1970s. The Base of Coorg Whitewater Rafting lies on the banks of KKR river in PONYA Estate on GONICOPAL - BIRUNANI Road (see route map below) to reach after 31kms drive from Gonicopal. The Short trip is conducted in the mid section and will take two hours to cover a stretch over 2 km, with an hour spent on briefing and drill, and the run to last another hour. You will be running through many rapids and few of them are named (as you enter) The Morning Coffee, The Grasshopper, Ramba Samba, The Wicked Witch and finally The Big Bang.
